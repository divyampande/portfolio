Institute of Mechanics · Technische Universität Berlin, Germany

Research Intern — Non-Linear Structural Analysis via PINNs & KANs

May 2024 – Aug 2024 · DAAD WISE Fellowship · Python

This was my first real exposure to research at a European institution, and it pushed me in directions I hadn't anticipated. The core challenge: can neural networks replace the mesh entirely in structural simulations? Turns out, with the right architecture, they can — and dramatically faster.

  • Replaced the mesh generation step of FEM entirely with a physics-informed neural network — the kind of thing that sounds simple until you're actually debugging it at 2am.
  • Integrated Kolmogorov-Arnold Network (KAN) spline layers to handle the nastier non-linearities in hyperelastic materials — something standard MLPs genuinely struggle with.
  • Achieved a 90% reduction in convergence iterations over the baseline architecture — a result that surprised even the supervisors.
  • Validated everything against FEM benchmarks on ANSYS, Abaqus, and FEniCS, landing within 0.5% L2 error across all test cases.

Adaptive Vertical Axis Wind Turbine (VAWT)

Jan 2024 – Apr 2024 · AAKRUTI'24 · Dassault Systèmes

The classic problem with Darrieus rotors is they won't self-start. Our fix: borrow the self-starting drag torque of a Savonius rotor and combine the two. Add a variable-pitch mechanism on top and you have something that works across a much wider range of wind conditions — we modelled several blade configurations to back it up.
